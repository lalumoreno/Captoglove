◆ BleScanType

enum GSdkNet.BLE.BleScanType
strong

Relation of speed / battery drain of scanning.

Enumerator
Background 

Slowest but battery saving scan mode. For windows scan is triggered every 20 seconds.

LowPowered 

Slow scan mode with relatevely low battery drain. For windows scan is triggered every 15 seconds.

Balanced 

Balanced mode between speed and battery drain. For windows scan is triggered every 10 seconds.

LowLatency 

Fast scan mode but battery draining. For windows scan is triggered every 5 seconds.
